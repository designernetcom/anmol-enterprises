About Anmol Enterprises

Established in 2006 in Pune, Maharashtra, Anmol Enterprises is a trusted name in the manufacturing, trading and wholesale supply of premium-quality packaging solutions. We specialize in the production of BOPP Self-Adhesive Tapes, Printed BOPP Tapes, Strapping Patti (PP Box Strapping Rolls), Wonder Tapes, Stretch Films, Air Bubble Rolls, Packaging Materials and Strapping Machines. With a strong commitment to quality, innovation and customer satisfaction, we deliver reliable packaging products that cater to the diverse requirements of industries such as logistics, manufacturing, e-commerce, pharmaceuticals, food processing and retail. Every product is manufactured using high-grade raw materials and advanced production techniques to ensure superior strength, durability and performance.

Under the visionary leadership of our Managing Director, Mr. Vaman Gaikwad, Anmol Enterprises has earned a reputation as one of the leading BOPP Tape Manufacturers and Strapping Patti Manufacturers in Pune. Our dedication to maintaining exceptional quality standards, competitive pricing and timely delivery has enabled us to build long-lasting relationships with clients across Maharashtra and India. In addition to manufacturing, we also offer a comprehensive range of packaging products, including Thermal Paper Rolls, Plain Paper Rolls, Manufacturing Rolls and other industrial packaging materials. With over 20 years of industry expertise, we continue to provide cost-effective, customized and dependable packaging solutions that help businesses protect, secure and streamline their packaging operations.
